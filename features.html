We used LDA on the Hurricane Harvey dataset to gain some insight into possible different topics that people might have been talking about in their tweets.

Several combinations of parameters were used and we also tried running this with the entire dataset of 400,000 tweets but this ended up being too computationally expensive. So for analyzing the tweets, we used these base values for parameters that allowed us to achieve manageable results:

  • Tweets: 8400
  • Topics: 3
  • Passes: 15

Topic 1 looks like it could be related to news about where the hurricane currently is- let's call it "News" and these tweets are most likely from news/weather agencies.

Topic 2 seems to be from the common people who are affected by the hurricane. These looks like calls for help and could also be from people active in relief efforts. Let's call this "Relief Effort"
